About Me

I work as a Software Engineer for Lockheed Martin in Colorado where I have had the opportunity to work on cutting edge satellite and aircraft technology. Currently, I am on the Integration, Verification, and Test team and primarily develop Python and C++ software to test the network device my team is designing for the F-35. Before moving to CO, I earned my BS in Computer Engineering from the University of Nebraska, Lincoln.

As a Software Engineer my greatest passion is developing products that help and improve the lives of others. I enjoy automating complex tasks using Python. With the work that I do, I hope to always be learning new things and honing my skills.

In my free time I enjoy photography, gaming, building mechanical keyboards and PCs, and spending time with my two cats.
